About TreeXplain

TreeXplain uses satellite imagery and explainable AI to detect and explain deforestation patterns. Our platform provides transparent insights into forest changes, helping researchers, policymakers, and conservationists understand and address deforestation.

  • Satellite Data: Sentinel-2, Landsat, and more
  • AI Model: U-Net for deforestation detection
  • Explainable AI: SHAP, GradCAM, Integrated Gradients
  • Open Source: Free and open for everyone
